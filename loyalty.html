Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the Crown & Anchor Society?

You're automatically enrolled in the Crown & Anchor Society after completing your first cruise with any Royal Caribbean Group brand. No registration required - your membership starts with your first sailing!

Do points expire?

No, your cruise points never expire. Once earned, they remain in your account permanently, allowing you to progress through tiers at your own pace.

Can I combine points from different brands?

Yes! Points earned on Royal Caribbean International, Celebrity Cruises, and Silversea all count toward your Crown & Anchor Society status. One loyalty program, three amazing brands.

How do I check my current tier status?

Log in to your account on RCG Connect to view your current tier, points balance, and progress toward the next level. You can also check your status through the mobile app or by contacting guest services.

What happens when I reach a new tier?

Your new tier status is effective immediately on your next cruise. You'll receive a welcome email with details about your new benefits, and your membership card will reflect your updated tier level.
